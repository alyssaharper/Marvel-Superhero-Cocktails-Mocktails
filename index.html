Welcome!

Have you always wanted to have your Hulk-themed party and want to make the perfect green drink? Or maybe your kid wants a Spiderman-themed birthday party, and you want to make some fantastic drinks. Well, you're in luck; this website is for you. This Marvel-themed cocktail website includes various beverages associated with four characters, including Black Widow, Captain America, Hulk, and Spiderman. Included with a name and picture of the drink, you also get a list of ingredients and the recipe for the ones you want to try at home. There is also a non-alcoholic or a ‘Mocktail’ version of each Cocktail; that way, your kids can enjoy the drink with you. Do you like a drink but want to look at others? No problem, just save your favorites and keep it moving.
